Ecommerce Sales Dashboard Insights:

1. Overall Performance:
   - Total Sales Amount: 438K
   - Total Profit: 37K
   - Total Quantity Sold: 5615
   - Average Order Value: 121K

2. Top States by Sales:
   - Maharashtra leads significantly, followed by Madhya Pradesh and Uttar Pradesh.

3. Product Category Insights:
   - Clothing accounts for 63% of total quantity sold.
   - Electronics and Furniture follow with 21% and 17%, respectively.

4. Monthly Profit Trends:
   - Highest profits observed in January and December.
   - Losses recorded in June and July.

5. Customer Analysis:
   - Harivansh and Madhav are the top customers by total purchase amount.

6. Payment Mode Distribution:
   - COD is the most used payment method (44%), followed by UPI and Debit Card.

7. Sub-Category Profit:
   - Printers generated the highest profit.
   - Bookcases and Sarees also performed well.

Conclusion:
The dashboard highlights strong sales in clothing and high profits in printers. Maharashtra is the top-performing state, and customer-specific targeting (Harivansh & Madhav) could improve retention. There is an opportunity to reduce losses during mid-year months (June & July).
